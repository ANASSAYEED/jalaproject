Webpage with Multiple Background Images

This section has two background images.

The first is positioned at the top-left and the second is at the bottom-right.

Each image has a defined size and does not repeat.

This is a simple webpage to demonstrate the usage of multiple background images in CSS. By applying background images in CSS, we can create visually interesting elements that combine multiple graphical layers. Each background image can have its size, position, and behavior specified independently.

In this example, two placeholder images are used: one larger and placed at the top-left, and a smaller one at the bottom-right. The size and position of these background images are customized using the `background-size` and `background-position` properties.
